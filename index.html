Botswana Mercantile Exchange
Data Opportunity

The launch of BMX creates a high-value moment: new commodity data streams, new market participants, and urgent compliance requirements. Squared can lead with a full data compliance and intelligence playbook.

1. BMX Compliance & Education Partner

Deliver DPA compliance templates, onboarding kits, and "Data Literacy for Commodities Trading" workshops for members, brokers, and cooperatives.

2. Commodity Intelligence Dashboard

Launch subscription analytics with price sentiment analysis, supply-demand maps, and anonymized trader behavior insights for decision-makers.

3. Research & Funding Proposal Support

Support ministries, unions, and NGOs with baseline studies, impact assessments, and proposal visuals for AfDB, EU, and UNDP opportunities.

4. Pre-Membership Data Audits

Prepare participants for BMX onboarding with audits on inventory data quality, reporting readiness, and DPA compliance controls.

5. Public "BMX Data Insights" Dashboard

Publish anonymized volume and price trend visuals branded by Squared to build authority and media visibility across Botswana.
